Basil Plant Care Guide

Soil for Basil

Well-drained, fertile soil.

Basil plants thrive in well-drained and fertile soil. Ensure good drainage for optimal root health.

Watering

Consistent and moderate watering.

Keep the soil consistently moist for basil plants, providing moderate watering for healthy growth.

Sunlight

Full sun exposure.

Plant basil in an area with full sunlight exposure for optimal flavor and essential oil production.

Benefits

Culinary herb.

Basil is a popular culinary herb, adding flavor to a variety of dishes and cuisines.

Repels pests.

Basil plants can help repel pests, making them a valuable companion in the garden.

Considerations

Pinch off flower buds for better leaf production; prune regularly for bushy growth; protect from extreme cold.
